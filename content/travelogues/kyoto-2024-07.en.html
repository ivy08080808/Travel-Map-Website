Kiyomizu-dera Temple

Kiyomizu-dera is one of Kyoto's oldest and most representative temples, founded in 778 AD. It is famous for its "Kiyomizu Stage," built entirely without nails, and the Otowa Waterfall. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site. We also visited this area wearing kimonos.

Yasaka Shrine

Yasaka Shrine is one of the most important shrines in Gion, known for its vermilion West Gate and as the origin of the Gion Matsuri.

Gion Matsuri

Gion Matsuri is Kyoto's grandest and oldest festival, originating in 869 AD, hosted by Yasaka Shrine. It is famous for its "Yamaboko Junko" (float procession) and is one of Japan's three great festivals. After visiting Yasaka Shrine, we were lucky to encounter this festival.
